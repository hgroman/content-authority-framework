# Voice Automated: Content Repository Structure & Strategy Plan
# (Client: Voice Automated | Framework: The Content Authority Framework™)

## Executive Summary

This plan transforms the Voice Automated blog from an unstructured archive into a strategic asset. It implements the "Content Authority Framework" to prove E-E-A-T to Google and create logical user journeys. The core of this strategy is to organize all content around four (4) designated "Strategic Hubs."

---

### Phase 1: Thematic Alignment (The "Compass")

All content must be filtered through these core strategic messages, which are derived from the "Voice Automated Strategic Content Guide & Core Identity."

* **Mission Focus:** All content must frame solutions (DMO, DAX Copilot) as directly addressing the crisis of administrative burden and clinician burnout.
* **Security Leadership:** All content must emphasize that VA solutions are **HITRUST CSF–certified**, positioning this as superior to the baseline **HIPAA** standard. This mitigates the $10.93M average cost of a healthcare data breach.
* **Proof Points & ROI:** All content must reinforce **quantifiable results** to prove value (e.g., 70% reduction in clinician burnout, 50% reduction in documentation time, 112% ROI).

---

### Phase 2: Content Categorization (Hierarchical Indexing)

All current and future content will be categorized as follows:

1.  **Dragon Medical One (DMO):** The "gold standard" cloud speech recognition.
    * *Tags:* Technical Release, EHR Integration, PowerMic Mobile, Windows 11.
2.  **Ambient AI Solutions:** DAX Copilot and the unified Dragon Copilot.
    * *Tags:* Multilingual Care, Specialty-Specific Models, Ambient Workflow.
3.  **Strategic Pillars:** Cross-product business outcomes and security.
    * *Tags:* HITRUST, ROI, Business Case, Implementation.
4.  **Technical/Legacy:** Support for older systems and hardware.
    * *Tags:* DMO Cost, PowerMic 4, Dragon Medical Practice Edition (DMPE).

### Phase 4: Technical & Lifecycle Tagging

To ensure content relevance and technical accuracy, posts must be tagged with their lifecycle status.

* **Version Tagging:** e.g., `DMO 2024.3`, `DAX Copilot 1.9`
* **Lifecycle Status:** e.g., `LTSR` (Long Term Service Release), `End-of-Life` (for DMPE 4)
* **Compatibility:** e.g., `Windows 11`, `Citrix`, `.NET 4.7.2`, `Epic Hyperdrive`
